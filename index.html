About Us!

The Company was established in June 5, 2020, as a result of the Port Reforms initiated by the Nigerian Government, with the aim to provide a high standard logistics service to Port users and Port Terminal Operators.

BCTL offers its services for shipments by air and sea including logistics for Customs formalities and handles transportation for all your imports to and exports from Nigeria. Our principal areas of activity are shipping agency, empty container holding-bay, import, export, haulage, warehousing and plant service. BCTL is able to ensure that it remains at the heart of the Nigerian shipping industry. It has since become one of the most advantageous holding-bay operator that directly located close to the port with 90% capacity above other empty container holding —bay in Lagos, Nigeria

BCTL EMPTY CONTAINER TERMINAL OPERATION.

BCTL Terminal has since then been undergoing a development process in order to comply with the highest standards of demands of our Customers — CMA CGM Shipping Line and Importers. Within this plan, we have purchased additional Cargo Handling Equipment (CHE) and installed IT systems and programmed to manage all gate in and gate out empty containers in real time. For us in BCTL, we believe that continuous improvement on the existing standards is the best way to achieving leadership over competition. Our dedicated and well trained staff are offering the best available service with a personal touch in order to satisfy our valuable customers. Beachland Consultium Terminal is in operation, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and 365 days a year.

BCTL WAREHOUSING

We offer warehousing services to major importers and exporters within the city and Tincan port. Our Warehousing Properties of 1000 square metres capacity is available for rent and storage of goods. We have been able to earn ourselves a respectable position among the leading providers of Warehousing Services. Our people are skilled professionals dedicated to your success hence; our Warehousing Service operates turnkey distribution facilities that are strategically located to serve our clients.

BCTL EXPORT

The Company have been duly registered to carry out the business of exportation.; hence, we have commenced our export chain business. The Export operation is now expanding our opportunities to extend our shipping busine to major exporters of Cocoa beans, cashew nuts, cassava and other exportable items. Our Warehouse located within the Terminal is an added advantage to our Customers to store export products before the arrival of vessel with dedicated and well trained staff, labour and stuffing equipments.

BCTL BONDED TERMINAL.

Our Customs Bonded Terminal services was approved by Nigeria Customs services and it will give logistics advantage being located within the port area, there easy access to vessel in terms of quick evacuation of laden containers . The Import Bonded terminal is well equipped with four 45 tones capacity containers handling plants, IT system, well trained staff and security services is being currently managed by Nigerian Police, Port Command for the best terminal services with a personal touch in order to satisfy the Nigerian importers and port stakeholders.

BCTL CLEARING AND FORWARDING AGENCY SERVICES.

We have registered with Nigeria Customs Services as one of the Clearing and Forwarding Agency in Nigeria, The Clearing and Forwarding Agency services give us logistics advantage being an indigenous Company to further expand our services to Honeywell Flour Plc and other Nigeria numerous Importers. The office is well equipped with IT system and dedicated and well trained staff for the best shipping service in order to have quick access to the port operation that will finally bring satisfaction to Customers and port stakeholders

BCTL LOGISTICS AND PLANT OPERATIONS.

BCTL Terminal is located directly on Union Dicon Salt Terminal, which afforded us advantage of Providing logistics and plant services to immediately handle numerous plants needs. Our Logistics and plant services have since then been undergoing a development process in order to comply with the highest standards of demands of our customer— CMA CGM Nigeria Shipping Company. Within this plan, we have purchased additional Cargo Handling Equipment. Our dedicated and well trained staff are also not left out in offering the best available service in order to satisfy our valuable customer. In order to satisfy our customer, plants are dedicated for 24 hours a day operation.
