About Us!

The name of the company is Beachland Consultium Terminal Limited (BCTL). We are Located at Beachland Estate, beside Rahamaniyya Oil & Gas Apapa, Lagos, Nigeria. The Company was established on June 5, 2020, as a result of the Port Reforms initiated by the Nigerian Government, with the aim to provide a high standard logistics service to Port users and Port Terminal Operators.

BCTL offers its services for shipments by air and sea including logistics for Customs formalities and handles transportation for all your imports to and exports from Nigeria. Our principal areas of activity are shipping agency, empty container holding-bay, import, export, haulage, warehousing and plant service. BCTL is able to ensure that it remains at the heart of the Nigerian shipping industry. It has since become one of the most advantageous holding-bay operator that is directly located close to the port with 90% capacity above other full import & empty container holding —bays in Lagos, Nigeria Our staff has been trained to give Customers first-class services. Our operation department is renowned for its personalized and professional approach to their job which is acknowledged by our satisfied customers. We have fully entrenched ourselves in this business as a service to our various clients. The Company's unwavering continual dedication to quality and standard services has earned it continuous patronage by clients. We have good working relationship with the national and international communities and our Company has properly positioned itself to meet the challenges of the industry. The Company identifies customers' needs, review services and make value-adding contribution to clients businesses.
